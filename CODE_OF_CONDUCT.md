# Code of Conduct

Our AAAP community is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone. To maintain a healthy project ecosystem, we ask all contributors to follow these guidelines:

* **Be Respectful:** Value different ideas and perspectives. Constructive feedback is encouraged, but keep it focused on the technology and the protocol, not on individuals.
* **Be Collaborative:** We are building this together. Be open to suggestions and support others in their contributions.
* **Use Inclusive Language:** Please use professional and inclusive language in all communications, issues, and pull requests.
* **Zero Tolerance for Harassment:** Harassment, discriminatory language, or any behavior that creates an uncomfortable environment will not be tolerated and may result in being banned from the project.

By participating in this project, you agree to uphold these standards. Thank you for helping us keep the AAAP community a great place to innovate!
